--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Let Eclipse do the typing
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Eclipse comes with Code Assist/Code (ctrl+space) completion. 

If you need a getter/setter, type get/set somewhere in your 
class (outside of a method declaration) and use Content Assist (ctrl+space)to 
let Eclipse create the method for you.
